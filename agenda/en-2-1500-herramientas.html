Tool for building action plans for Open Data (ODRA)


Speakers

Maurice McNaughton | The University of the West Indies | JM
Eva Clemente | Banco Mundial
Jeff Kaplan | Socrata | US


Moderator

Amparo Ballivain


Anfitriones Co-hosts
World Bank

ODRA is a tool designed by the World Bank to help assess if a country or city is ready to successfully implement an Open Data strategy. This panel will show the advantages and opportunities of this process from the perspective of consultants and public officials que have used it.
