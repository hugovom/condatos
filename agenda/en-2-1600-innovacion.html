Public Challenges: opening procurement


Speakers

Victor Lagunes | México Digital Presidencia de la República | MX
Jorge Soto | México Digital Presidencia de la República | MX
Kiyoshi Tsuru | CIDE | MX,


Moderator

Jorge Soto


Anfitriones Co-hosts
USAID

Promoting innovation in digital services by democratizing government procurement is an important challenge. Public Challenges seeks to be a meeting point between tech entrepreneurs and ministries in need of innovative solutions. This panel will showcase some of the challenges the Mexican government has launched as well as some of the participant’s proposals.
