Privacy in the face of Openness


Speakers

Fernando Esponda | ITAM | MX
Gabriela Barrantes | Universida de Costa Rica | CR
Alejandro Noriega | MIT Human Dynamics Lab | US


Moderator

Claudio Ruiz


In spite of the benefits generated by opening data, there are important risks that must be considered. This panel will open a debate on the balance between open data, privacy, and personal data.
