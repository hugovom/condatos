13:00-14:00 PM


Evaluation of Open Data Policies


Speakers

Allison O’Beirne | Gobierno de Canadá | CA
Antonio Acuña | Gobierno del Reino Unido | UK
Bárbara Ubaldi | Organización para la Cooperación y el Desarrollo Económicos
Carlos Iglesias | SBC4D


Moderator

John Wonderlich*


Anfitriones Co-hosts
OCDE

The aim of this panel is to discuss alternative methods to assess the government’s capacity to adopt Open Data policies and the importance of measuring the impact and value creation using Open Data.
