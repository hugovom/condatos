Open Government Plan: a roadmap for the governments of the region

CEPAL


October 1st from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM (to be confirmed)


Its aimed at providing concepts and practical tools to enable participants to develop and implement a strategy/open government plan for their organizations, with an emphasis in the field of open data and reuse of public sector information. It is mainly addressed to public managers, strategists for open government and open to all levels of public administration in the region (central, regional, and local government). Representatives of civil society and other stakeholders in the ecosystem of open government / data open data are also welcome.
