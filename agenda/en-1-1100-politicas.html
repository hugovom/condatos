Open Data for Development


Speakers

José M. Alonso | World Wide Web Foundation
Vagner Diniz | World Wide Web Consortium | BR

Moderator

Caroline Burle


Anfitriones Co-hosts
ILDA | W3C | IDRC | AVINA

The purpose of this session is to explore OD4D’s new platform: how it works, the use of standards, semantic web and linked data, and the interaction of government, society and business with the platform. We will also discuss the emerging impacts of open data in developing countries and the efforts of the Latin American Open Data Initiative to promote research, ownership and use of open data.
